Privacy Policy for LandlordPro

1. About LandlordPro

LandlordPro provides users(landlords) with tools to manage rental agreements. The user(landlord) themselves registers and processes personal data, and LandlordPro only functions as a technical platform.

2. Who is responsible for the data?

  • The user(landlord) is the data controller for the personal data they register.
  • LandlordPro is a data processor and stores information on behalf of the user(landlord).

3. What data is processed?

The user(landlord) can register the following information about themselves and tenants:

  • Name, date of birth, address, phone number, and email of both the user(landlord) and tenants
  • Rental agreements and payment history
  • Any expenses related to the rental agreement
  • Uploaded contracts and documents

The landlord is responsible for sending the generated GDPR document to the tenant upon registration.

4. How is this data used?

Personal data is registered and used only for managing the rental agreement.

5. Deletion and storage of data

  • The user(landlord) can delete tenant data at any time.
  • The data is stored as long as necessary for management purposes.
